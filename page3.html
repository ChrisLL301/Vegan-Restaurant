Why we do what we do


The three of us have a multitude of reasons why we chose veganism for both our lifestyle choices and for this restaurant. These reasons brought us closer to not only eachother, but to YOU as well!! Not only is veganism socio-economically better for us, but it takes way less space (1/3rd of what animal agriculture needs). To help the environment even more, we offer our amazing menu with a farm-to-table system. Owning farms not too far from here, we directly overview our entire food delivery process and make sure its always environmentally friendly. We love animals as well, and as to stop the slaughter they experience, we stand up for them. Although many institutes and farms have more ethical ways to get meat, the vast majority still uses cruel and unethical methods to produce their meats and treat their animals, factoring veganism as a great alternative in order to help these animals. Lastly its important to know that both American and British organizations approve of our vegan methods as you do it properly, in order to get each nutrient for your body. People with low blood pressure, cholesterol can be helped by this diet, and veganism can also prevent certain cancers. So as long as you follow proper guidance, any age can start and live a fulfilling life with a vegan diet and help not only their bodies but our beautiful nature surrounding us!!
