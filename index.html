Welcome to our veggie restaurant! Your health is our priority.


OUR MISSION STATEMENT: Similar to yourself, we as a business strive for better sustainability for our planet. Unfortunately, 16.5% of greenhouse emissions are due to animal agriculture. We also know that 90% of the Amazon Rainforest's deforestation is due to livestock production. The usage of land, water, and emissions all rise in the wake of animal agriculture, and these issues pose a threat to the Earth’s climate. Vegetarianism is more sustainable for the Earth’s future, and we’d like to remain standing for years to come, so we ask for your support as we continue to venture out in our business.
