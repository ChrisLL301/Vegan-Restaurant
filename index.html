Welcome to our veggie restaurant! Your health is our priority.


Hello there dear vegan enthusiast!! Similar to yourself, we as a business strive for better sustainability for our planet. We can understand that veganism helps the environment and how normal animal agriculture harms it. Unfortunatley 16.5% of greenhouse emissions are due to animal agriculture. We also know that 90% of the Amazon Rainforest's deforestation is due to livestock production. Usage of land, water, and emissions all rise to raise dairy animals for their products which for the most part these animals are not taken care of properly. These all can be issues as they change the earths climate, destroy animals' lives and it doesn't support the future. This is why we are here!! We don't want the guilt of ruining our future so instead we will come up and cook up some real tasty meals without the added seasoning of despair and grief! Get ready for more, get ready for Veggie Bowl!!
